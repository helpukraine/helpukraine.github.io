Help Ukraine Resist

Are you looking for ways to help Ukraine? Can you volunteer time, money, skills? Here are some ideas for you.

Want to donate directly to the Ukrainian Army?

You can donate by the credit card, G Pay or bank transfer to the special account of the NBU.


Want to donate for non-lethal equipment?

CO "International Charitable Foundation "Come Back Alive"

Focus: "Come Back Alive" supplies technology, training, and ammunition. After arriving home from the front line of defense, our troops receive support with sports rehabilitation and the development of veteran-run businesses. Banking details.

Charity fund ARMY SOS

Focus: Army SOS manages purchases of necessary ammunition, shields, uniforms, intercommunication and reconnaissance facilities, and food supply. Banking details.


Are you willing to help with the medical treatment of those wounded in action?

Donate the charity fund “Hospitallers”. Banking details.


Support children of war in Ukraine!

You can do so by donating to the Voices of Children Foundation using the credit card, paypal, or bank transfer.


Can you offer an accommodation for refugees?

If you are willing to offer an accommodation for refugees from Ukraine, you can do so on the following sites:
  • Airbnb.
  • host4ua.
  • EU4UA.
  • Shelter for UA.
  • Host a Sister, Ukrainian Refugee Network.
  • Prykhystok (in Ukrainian only, but works with google translate).
  • Dopomogai (in Ukrainian only, but works with google translate).
  • Poland: uasos.
  • Germany: Unterkunft-Ukraine, Wunderflats.
  • Romania: dopomoha.ro (not limited to housing only).
  • Hungary: Help for Ukrainians (not limited to housing only).
  • Lithuania: StrongTogether.

Can you provide a job for Ukrainian refugees?

Please register your job offer on jobs4ukr.

Do you have a medical training? Help hospitals in Ukraine.

The Ministry of Health of Ukraine invites you to work in medical institutions in Ukraine during this difficult time. Contacts:
  • Mariana Sluzhynska, +38 050 315 8418, ukrainemedhelp@gmail.com

Do you have excellent IT skills?

  • Join the IT Army Ukraine.
  • Are you a keyboard warrior? Want to share your IT skills with organisations in Ukraine? Contact Tech To the Rescue.
  • Are you active on social media? Share evidences of Russian crimes in Ukraine.

Do you think your government should do more?

  • Take part in rallies: interactive map.
  • Write to your MP urging more action from your government.

Do you wish to provide a humanitarian aid for Ukraine?

  • Do you want to help Ukraine with medicines or something else? Check the list of needs of the Help Ukraine! center, buy and send this to the center.
  • If you already have a humanitarian aid and are looking for logistics, Crisis Logistics Center has a pool of reliable carriers. Add a request for transportation and the Center will connect you with a carrier ready for delivery.

Do you want to support Ukrainians directly?

Book a room via Airbnb in Ukraine. We do not urge you to stay in Ukraine during the war, however your booking will support people, who are mostly left without income at this moment.

Should western companies still do bussiness as usual in Russia?

A few hundreds of companies have withdrawn from Russia - but some remain. On this page you will find a list of companies that have withdrawn and a list of those, which still remain. Write a message to one of those companies and explain why you think it is inappropriate to remain on the Russian market.

Do you have friends in Russia? Talk with them.

Vladimir Putin has lied to Russians for decades. He is lying about Ukraine now, and he has blacked out sources of truth.
